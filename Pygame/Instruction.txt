1. Press arrow up to jump
2. Press arrow left to move left
3. Press arrow right to move right
4. Press space button to kill enemies
5. After killing 10 enemies level 2 will start
6. After level 2 boss will come
7. If you get close to the boss you will die
8. Level, health and remaining life shows at the left top
9. If you kill an enemy health boast will top up at the place
10. After you die 3 times new game start option will arise
11. Hero, enemy and boss has different symbol
